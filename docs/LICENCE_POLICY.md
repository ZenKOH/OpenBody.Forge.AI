# Licence Policy

No 3D model, texture, rig or motion clip may be committed as a distributable asset until these fields are documented:

1. Source URL or source note.
2. Licence text.
3. Attribution requirement.
4. Redistribution statement.
5. Modification history.
6. Safety review status.

## Bundling policy

Mixamo, AMASS, BABEL and similar sources may be useful for workflow or research, but their assets must not be bundled until redistribution rights are reviewed.

## Recommended default

Use MakeHuman official exports first, but still verify each exported clothing, hair and texture dependency before committing app assets.
