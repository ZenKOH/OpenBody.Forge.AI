Pipeline

From open model source to browser avatar

1. SourceMakeHuman, VALID, Anny route, local GLB/FBX/OBJ, or manual model.
2. Licence AIScore redistribution, attribution and dependency risk.
3. BlenderImport, clean, rig, retarget and export.
4. ValidateRig readiness, motion quality and safety review.
5. IntegrateExport GLB package and browser runtime manifest.

Safety and rights boundary
OpenBody Forge AI does not diagnose, prescribe, assess a user's movement, or bundle third-party human models or mocap clips without verified redistribution rights. The app helps prepare educational avatar assets and manifests for review.
